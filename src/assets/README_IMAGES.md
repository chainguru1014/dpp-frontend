# Image Assets Required

Please add the following images to this directory (`frontend/src/assets/`):

## Required Images:

1. **app-store-badge.png**
   - White background with gray border
   - "Download on the App Store" button
   - Apple logo on the left, text on the right
   - Recommended size: ~250x83px or similar aspect ratio

2. **google-play-badge.png**
   - White background with gray border
   - "Get it on Google Play" button
   - Google Play logo on the left, text on the right
   - Recommended size: ~250x83px or similar aspect ratio

3. **yometel-logo.png**
   - Already exists in this directory
   - Used at the top center of the public product page

## Image Sources:

You can download the official badges from:
- App Store: https://developer.apple.com/app-store/marketing/guidelines/
- Google Play: https://play.google.com/intl/en_us/badges/

Or use the images you provided in the screenshots.

## File Format:
- PNG format recommended
- Transparent or white background
- High resolution for crisp display
